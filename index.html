GyanTaa AI is an intelligent career-guidance web application designed especially for students who have just passed 10th or 12th grade and are unsure about their future path. The platform helps students discover the right career, build a professional resume, and follow a clear roadmap by suggesting the exact skills, projects, and learning steps required for their chosen field. Instead of confusion and random decisions, GyanTaa AI provides structured guidance, making career planning simple, practical, and student-friendly. The goal is to empower students with clarity, confidence, and direction at the very beginning of their professional journey.

Challenges Faced by Todayâ€™s Students

Many students struggle after completing 10th or 12th due to a lack of proper guidance and reliable resources. Career confusion is one of the most common challenges, as students often feel unsure about which stream, domain, or career path truly matches their interests and strengths. Even after selecting a field, the absence of proper learning roadmaps creates uncertainty about what skills to learn, the correct order to follow, and the best platforms to rely on.

Another major issue students face is resume building. Without prior experience, many students find it difficult to create a professional resume or understand what information recruiters actually look for. Along with this, learning resources related to skills, projects, and career preparation are scattered across multiple platforms, making the learning process overwhelming and unfocused.

Most importantly, students lack early-stage mentorship and career guidance. Without access to mentors or counselors at the right time, students often make decisions based on confusion, peer pressure, or incomplete information, which affects their long-term career growth.
